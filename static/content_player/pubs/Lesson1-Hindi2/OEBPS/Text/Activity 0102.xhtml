कहानी का समय : पहली बैठक

  • पहली बैठक

अब, कहानी को सुनने के लिए इस वीडियो को देखने के लिए। आप उपशीर्षक पढ़ संवादों का सावधानी से पालन करने के लिए कर सकते हैं।
नीचे दिए गए वीडियो में क्लिक करें और कहानी को देखते हैं।

वीडियो ट्रांसक्रिप्ट

Sound of bus stopping at station

Bus conductor: Chotapur! Chotapur! Hey boy, your father said that you have to get down at this stop.

Zo: Oh, okay. I'll get my bags and get down here. Thank you, conductor. Bye.

Zo gets down from the bus and Kanasu, on her cycle, comes crashing into him.

Zo: (surprised and in pain) Owww! Can’t you see where you are going?

Kanasu: (equally surprised) You did not see where you were going! Look at what you've done to my cycle! The handle bar is all bent.

Zo: Look at what you've done to me! My elbows are bruised.

Sahir sees this scuffle and walks towards them.

Sahir: Kanasu, are you okay?

Kanasu:I'm not hurt. Who's this boy?

Sahir: (to Zo) And you? Are you fine?

Zo:I’m fine. These are just scratches on my elbow.

Sahir:(admonishingly)Kanasu, this was your fault. Don’t shout at him for something you know you have done.

Kanasu:(sulking) But...! Okay...fine. Yes, it was my fault. I’m sorry.

Zo:That's Okay.

Sahir:Hi, my name is Sahir. I don't think we have met before. Are you visiting someone here in Chotapur?

Zo: Hi, I'm Zo. I have come from Badapur. I'm waiting for my grandmother to pick me up.

Kanasu:Hello, Zo. I'm Kanasu. I've heard so much about Badapur. Did you go to school there? Which class are you in? Tell me more about Badapur. Oh! I can see Aunty coming towards us! Hi, Aunty!

Zo: That's my Grandma!

Grandma:Hi, Zo! I’m sorry I'm late. I’m getting older by the day and my knees are getting weaker. You have grown at least 2 inches since I last saw you! What happened to your elbows?

Zo:I had a small fall but I'm fine. Unfortunately, the handlebar of Kanasu’s cycle is bent.

Grandma: I see that you've already met Kanasu and Sahir. Zo can help you fix your cyle, Kanasu. He's really good with repairing things. Why don't you both join us for a cup of tea?

Sahir:Sure, Aunty. That’s a good idea. Come, Zo, let me help you carry that bag. Kanasu, you can walk with the cycle.

Zo:No thanks Sahir. It's alright. It's not heavy.

Kanasu:You can put the other bag on the cycle, Zo. Let’s go!

यदि आप फिर से कहानी को सुनने के लिए चाहते हैं आप वीडियो पुनरावृत्ति कर सकते हैं ।
